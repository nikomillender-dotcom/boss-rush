*Turn-based RPG climbs 1,000 floors across ten themed arcs; playable now as a PWA with no login*

**[CITY, May 25, 2026]** — [Developer/studio name] today released **Boss Rush**, a turn-based boss-gauntlet RPG playable instantly at **https://boss-rush-six.vercel.app**. Players choose a feline class, upgrade stats at a persistent camp, and battle through ten themed 100-floor arcs—from suburban terriers to cosmic hounds—before confronting **Doggod: All Seeing Eye** at floor 1,000 and continuing into scaling Free Play.

Unlike idle “boss rush” titles flooding storefronts, Boss Rush emphasizes readable JRPG commands—Fight, Skills, and Defend—with manual control on boss floors, streak-based coin rewards, and a retreat mechanic that forfeits only the current run’s earnings.

**Key features:**

- **Four base classes** — Tabby Knight, Whisker Mage, Shadow Cat, and Whisker Cleric—each with distinct skill kits and unlock gates tied to progression depth  
- **Prestige combo classes** — including Spellclaw Knight (Warrior + Mage at floor 100 on both parents) with expanded skill rosters  
- **Ten themed enemy blocks** — human, monster, hell, space (with planet bosses), alien, mirror (flipped sprites), heaven, olympus, pantheon, and angelic  
- **Capstone bosses every 100 floors** — notably **Guy With A Dog** at floor 100 and **Doggod** at floor 1000  
- **PWA support** — install to home screen on iOS/Android for full-screen, offline-capable play after first load  
- **Original audio** — CC0 music loops by Kenney.nl; no copyrighted game rips
