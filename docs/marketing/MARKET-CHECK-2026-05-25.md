## TikTok & short-form (2026)

### What changed

- TikTok remains a top **discovery** surface for games (~1.6B MAU; platform reports high gamer engagement), but **free organic reach is tighter** than 2023–2024. Expect to **earn** distribution with hooks, then optionally boost winners.  
- **Max 5 hashtags** per post — choose deliberately.  
- **Cross-post** every clip to Reels and YouTube Shorts; remove TikTok watermark on other platforms.  
- **Paid TikTok UA** is a weak default for indies (long funnel: view → bio link → Steam/web). Organic + creator repurposing tends to outperform small ad spends.

**Sources:**

- [Cloutboost — TikTok game marketing 2026](https://www.cloutboost.com/blog/tiktoks-changing-landscape-for-game-marketing-in-2026-what-developers-need-to-know)

## Boss-rush / turn-based niche

### Gap Boss Rush can own

Most 2025–2026 “boss rush” titles on Steam skew **idle/incremental**, **tactical grid**, or **short boss-only platformers**. Few combine:

- **JRPG-style turn commands** (Fight / Skills / Defend)
- **Camp shop meta** between runs
- **100-floor themed arcs × 10** + endless free play
- **Browser-first, no account**

That positions you as a **pick-up-and-climb** mobile/web RPG rather than a roguelike deckbuilder.

| Title | Release | Overlap | Differentiation |
|-------|---------|---------|-----------------|
| [Bossfight Tactics](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1635710/Bossfight_Tactics/) | Aug 2025 | Turn-based, tactical | Item-synergy roguelike; no endless floor ladder |

| [Deified](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3623530/Deified/) | EA Apr 2026 | Turn-based bosses | Relic-slot tactics; 7 bosses, serious tone |
| [2048 Bosses](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3868820/) | Coming 2026 | “Boss” in name | 2048 puzzle combat; different genre |

**Naming:** “Boss Rush” is descriptive but crowded. In store copy, lead with **cat vs dog** and **1000 floors** before the genre label.
