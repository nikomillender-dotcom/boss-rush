Privacy

Local play: Progress is stored in your browser (localStorage). We do not receive it unless you sign in and enable cloud save.

Processors: Vercel (hosting), Lemon Squeezy (license keys), Stripe (payments), Supabase (accounts and optional cloud saves). Each has its own privacy policy.

Analytics: Optional anonymous game events may be logged server-side. No ad trackers in the game client.

Contact the developer listed on the itch.io or Vercel page for data requests.
